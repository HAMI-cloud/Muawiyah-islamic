Daily Quranic Verse

Surah Al-Baqarah (2:286)

لَا يُكَلِّفُ اللَّهُ نَفْسًا إِلَّا وُسْعَهَا
"Allah does not burden a soul beyond that it can bear..."

This blessed verse reminds us that Allah (SWT) knows our limits and never tests us beyond our capacity. Every hardship comes with ease, and patience during trials leads to great rewards. This is a promise from Allah that with every difficulty comes relief, and we should always turn to Him in prayer and supplication.

Hadith of the Day

The strong believer is better and more beloved to Allah than the weak believer, while there is good in both.

- Sahih Muslim 2664

This hadith teaches us that true strength lies in faith, good character, and reliance on Allah. The "strong believer" refers to one who is strong in faith, knowledge, and righteous actions. We should strive to improve ourselves in both religious and worldly matters while maintaining complete trust in Allah's plan.
